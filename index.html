How To Use The Academy

Welcome to your private educational workshop. Here is how to get started:

  • Academy: Add students to your roster. Track their daily attendance and log individual subject grades here.
  • Lessons: Break down subjects into actionable tasks. Check them off as you progress, and log final grades.
  • Planner: Plan your day, set the focus timer, leave quick notes, and keep track of daily habits.
  • Playbook: Gamify executive function with 80+ offline games, a scoreboard, and a high-stakes timer.
  • Journal: Record thoughts and attach images. Everything is stored locally on your device.
  • Editor: Customize the background, colors, and sounds. Save your perfect setup to the Vault inside the editor.

📸 Pro Tip: The Daily Snapshot

When you are finished for the day, click the 📸 Snapshot button in the top navigation. The engine will instantly compile your current attendance, all student statuses, and your lesson progress, log it as a time-stamped text entry directly into your Private Journal, and automatically download your backup core data file.

🤖 Pro Tip: Activating the Academy Guide

1. Go to local notes and sign in with your Google account.
2. Click "Get " and create a new key.
3. Copy the key, open the Academy Guide from the top navigation in The Academy, paste it into the "" field, and click Save Key.
